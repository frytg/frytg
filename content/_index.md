# Hello, I'm Daniel,

or _Dan_ for short.
{.text-3xl .title-style}

I'm a **DevOps engineer** at [SWR Audio Lab](https://www.swr.de/unternehmen/audiolab/index.html) and a **hybrid athlete** chasing myself doing HYROX races in my spare time. This site is where I document the process of making both work.

![Photo collection of profile pictures](images/about/web-about-2024-q1-1@2x.webp)

## What I do

**At work**, I build and run backend infrastructure supporting platforms like [ARD Sounds](https://www.ardsounds.de/) and SWR's radio apps. From live metadata, voice assistants, audio delivery, to API systems serving billions of requests per month. I also work on the [responsible integration](https://www.swr.de/unternehmen/ki-guidelines-100.pdf) of [AI into public broadcasting](https://www.ard.de/die-ard/presse-kontakt/ard-pressemeldungen/2026/02-25-ard-popwellen-fuehren-regionalisierten-ki-service-fuer-wetter-und-verkehr-ein-100.html). I care about systems that are observable, sustainable, and built to last beyond my involvement.

**Outside of work**, I train in my free time — a mix of strength, running, cycling, and functional fitness. I chase my personal best in [HYROX](https://www.hyresult.com/athlete/daniel-freytag) races.

**On here**, I write about what I'm learning across both worlds. Training, building systems, staying consistent, and everyday life.

![Pictures of me at work and events](images/about/web-about-2024-q1-2@2x.webp)

## A few things about me

I'm based in southwest Germany. I value quality over quantity — in code, in training, in how I spend my time. The thread through everything: solving problems with digital or hybrid solutions.

Previously I worked in live and film productions, press photography, and web development — from chatbots nominated for [Deutscher Radiopreis](https://www.swr3.de/wir/deutscher-radiopreis-2019-swr3-dreimal-nominiert-100.html) to pandemic information platforms and virtual trade fairs.
