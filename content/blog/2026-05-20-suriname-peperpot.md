Further along our route, we landed in Peperpot, where we joined a bunch of Dutchies for a night walk through the Peperpot Nature Park – a place where having a guide makes total sense. Their trained eyes can both spot all sorts of camouflaged animals and also let you know which ones you should avoid if you were planning to continue your holiday...
