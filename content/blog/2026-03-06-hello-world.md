Hi there, it's been a while...

I set up this blog at the end of 2024 with the ambition of writing regularly about my work and personal projects.

Not like insanely long pieces, but something that's longer than a thought on [Mastodon](https://beoriginal.social/@FRYTG). Also something that's a bit more long-lived than a tweet.

I failed at that.

It was the common vicious cycle of "I want to write about topic xyz", but with a sprinkle of perfectionism, so a first draft (if I even wrote one) was never perfect and hence we made it through all of 2025 without a single post. Yay.

Meanwhile I enjoyed reading and following other people's blogs and even set up my own RSS reader again. Good ol' internet without algorithms!

Consider this a promise and a restart. I am aiming for one post per week to build some consistency. Feel free to tag along – for all the old-school nerds out there, there's an RSS feed link at the bottom of this page.

Be original. <3
